Starship SN15 Succesfull altitude flight test

06/05/21

Spacex yesterday tested the Starship SN15 and for the first time they were able to take it back to Earth in one piece!

This new protoype incorporated many upgrades that Spacex accumulated of previous launches and finally they have yielded amazing results.

The spaceship was able to self-approach to the landing platform and land smoothly to the surface. After that a moderately big flare of fire of methane gas started making us think that maybe it would explode again, but finally it turned out to be just a big fright.
