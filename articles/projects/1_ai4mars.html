AI4Mars

15/05/20

If you ever wondered how you can help the NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory in the quest to explore Mars, you can do it now! Terrain is one of the main obstacles of Mars for the rovers, you may know of Spirit the rover that got stuck in a pit ending its mission after 7 years of exploring Mars.

NASA scientists in a quest to make rovers able to indentify dangerous terrain by themselves are working with Machine Learning and to do it they need a LOT of training data to learn from.

The main problem is that we have huge datasets of mundane things but it doesnÂ´t have any utility when we talk about another planet!

That is where we enter in actionby labeling Curiosity Images and classifying the terrain as sand, consolidated soil, bedrock and big rocks.

With our help they are training an AI called SPOC to do deep learning for semantic image segmentation. This means that the AI model, once trained, will look at each pixel and predict which category it falls into in addition to how confident it is about that prediction.
